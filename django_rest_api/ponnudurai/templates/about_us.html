About Us

Learn about our heritage and commitment to excellence

Who We Are

Operating since the last 50 years, we are one of the top class leading Textile and jewellery in Southern Tamilnadu. Our products meets high quality standard authorised by BIS-hallmark. We have huge varieties of traditional designs and styles perfectly designed for special occasions. We tailor our range of jewellery as per our client's desire and specifications. We handcraft our jewellery in a wide array of options with respect to colour, designs and materials.

Why Us

Traditional Jewellery

We understand the significance of tradition in jewellery. Our collection seamlessly blends classic designs with modern aesthetics, ensuring timeless pieces that reflect the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship.

BIS Hallmark Quality

Quality is paramount at A.Ponnudurai Jewellers. All our pieces are BIS Hallmark certified, assuring authenticity and purity that you can trust.

Wide Range of Designs

Explore a plethora of designs at A.Ponnudurai Jewellers. From intricate motifs to contemporary styles, our diverse collection caters to every taste and occasion.

Low Waste

We prioritize sustainability by minimizing waste in our production processes, ensuring that every piece of jewellery is crafted with utmost efficiency and care for the environment.

Trendy Collections

Stay ahead of the fashion curve with our trendy collections. Our team of designers keeps a keen eye on the latest trends, curating jewellery pieces that are not only fashionable but also timeless.

Gold Purchase Scheme

Planning to invest in gold? Our gold purchase scheme offers a convenient and secure way to accumulate gold over time, helping you achieve your financial goals with ease.

One-Stop Destination

Whether you're in search of gold, silver, diamond jewellery, or exquisite vessels, A.Ponnudurai Jewellers is your one-stop destination. Explore our comprehensive range of jewellery and find the perfect piece to adorn every moment of your life.
